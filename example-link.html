RULES

THE FOLLOWING IS THE SERVER RULES

Server Rules

General

  1) Be nice and respectful to everybody, this is not a place for hatred, insults etc.
  2) Don’t spam, one message is enough.
  3) Only tag somebody or a group if it’s really necessary.
  4) Listen to all team members and moderators, they can ban you and they will.
  5) Any unnecessary or false report applications is not allowed (could end up with a ban if repeated) .
  6) Only English in chat.
  7) Use common sense.
  8) Staff impersonating is not allowed by any means.
  9) Please make sure all topics are appropriate for children.
      - Nothing can be shared that is NSFW, is relating to substance abuse, is disturbing or that displays a grave nature

  10) You cannot use your voice to annoy others in any VC (ex. voice changers/earrape).
  11) No interfering with moderator's duties:
      - Don't argue with them while they actively moderate
      - Don't troll with fake evidence
  12) Please keep your topics in the correct channel.

Support
  13) Wait patiently for an answer and don’t tag anybody.
  14) Don’t repeat your question unless you are asked for it.
  15) If you got a question, don't instantly go to Owner, ask players, ask Helpers, if they (Helpers) are going to lead you to higher staff members, then do it.
